Overview

Purpose

The site is a small business site providing website development to small business owners. The site will be easy to interpret and provide a portfolio of my website work for examples. The site will give future customers a way to contact me to begin the process of getting a bid and plan for their new website.

Audience

The primary audience will be small business owners who either do not have a website or have a website that needs updating and revamping.
